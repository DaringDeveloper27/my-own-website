My Story

My programming journey began at the age of 10 when my father enrolled me in a Robotics course. That is where I first fell in love with computer science, building simple hardware circuits and writing logic in C++. Working alongside electrical components like ultrasonic sensors and buzzers showed me how hardware and software bridge together to solve real-world problems.

While building robots was exciting, I found myself captivated by the core engineering behind the code. C++ was undeniably challenging at first, but its sheer power quickly made it my favorite language. After mastering the fundamentals, I applied my knowledge to build my first major software project: a terminal-based scientific calculator.

In the summer of 2023, I expanded my horizons by exploring Data Science, which introduced me to Python. To deepen my skillset, I committed to Dr. Angela Yu's rigorous 100 Days of Code with Python, followed by studying object-oriented development in Java through Abdul Bari on Udemy.

A transformative milestone in my education was diving into Harvard's renowned CS50x on edX during the summer of 2025. This intensive course solidified my command over C, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while deeply introducing me to crucial advanced computer science foundations like memory management, data structures, and low-level algorithms.

Today, I am channeling this momentum back into my favorite language, C++, using rigorous data structures and algorithms to solve complex programmatic challenges on LeetCode and design efficient software solutions.
