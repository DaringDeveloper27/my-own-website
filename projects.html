Stock Trading Simulator

This was my most recent project where I made a Stock Trading Simulator that prepares beginners for the real-world market. In this command-line program, you are given 'money' which you can choose to spend on tech stocks. Some of the companies include world leaders in technology such as Apple, NVIDIA, Google, Meta, and many more. After buying stocks, you can sell them and try to gain some profit. To simulate the price fluctuations of the real-life stock market, I have created a virtual "Market Engine" where the prices are determined based on Normal Distribution and the Mersenne Twister Algorithm. This project is developed using C++.

Adaptive Cruise Control

This was a project that relies on hardware provided by Moonprenuer's car kit. This project simulates a car on an automated driving system. This means that the car can travel on its own, similar to FSDs on Teslas. This project used hardware such as Ultrasonic sensor, and Motors. This project is written in the Arduino variant of C++.
